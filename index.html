Additional Information/FAQ

Can I pay at the event? - Yes! Just make sure it's the race day amount of $30. Unfortunately though, at the race we can only accept cash or checks.

Can donations to the clinic be made at the event? - Yes! Any cash or check donation at the 5k would be welcome and very appreciated!

Are shirts guarenteed with a race entry? - Yes and no. Any sign-up before March 10th includes a guarenteed T-shirt, after that they will be given out as supplies last.

Can I walk instead of run? - Yes! We welcome anyone to join us at this event, whether they can run an entire 5k or not.

Can an adult participate in the Fun Run (and vice versa)? - Unfortunately not. For the sake of organization we are only allowing those 12 and under to participate in the Fun Run and those 13 and over to participate in the 5k. Completion is not required though, so feel free to signup for the 5k and stop whenever you may need to.

Can I get a refund after signing up? - Unfortunately not. We will not offer refunds once a sign-up has been completed.

How do I get my race bag(tag, shirt, etc.)? - There will be a table in the area of the starting line.

Are strollers allowed? - Yes, but please be considerate of others competing in the 5k.

Are pets allowed? - Yes, but in the interest of other runners/walkers please keep them on a leash at all times

Our Purpose

The Free Clinic 5k was created to allow the Meridian community to participate in a health-related activity while also raising money and awareness for the Free Clinic of Meridian. All money raised with the 5k as well as any donations received at the event will be given to Clinic. The Free Clinic of Meridian provides free medical care for more than 1,300 members of our community who have no health insurance, are ineligible for Medicare or Medicaid, and earn less than 200% of the Federal Poverty Level. Additional donations can be made on the Clinic's website.
